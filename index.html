Privacy Policy for CAGR Calculator

Effective Date: 25-Sep-2025

Overview

CAGR Calculator ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension designed to help users calculate the Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of their investments quickly and easily.

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is (and is not) collected when you use the Extension.

Data Collection & Usage

  • No Personal Data Collected: The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.
  • No Tracking: The Extension does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies.
  • Local Processing: All calculations are performed locally on your device within the Chrome browser. No information is sent to external servers.

Permissions

The Extension may request minimal browser permissions required to function properly. These permissions are used solely for providing the core features of the Extension and are not used for data collection.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with or share data with third-party services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page. Continued use of the Extension after such updates will constitute your acceptance of the changes.
